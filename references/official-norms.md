## 选择规则

- Always include 2, 3, 7, 8, 9, 12, 14, and 15 for ordinary first submission.
- Include 4 when filing/备案/软著/版号/主体资料 are involved.
- Include 5 when short-video anchor, CPA, CPT, CPM, CPS, or达人推广 is involved.
- Include 10 when any advertising monetization exists.
- Include 11 when any in-app purchase, paid prop, recharge, or subscription exists.
- Include 13 when traffic promotion is configured.
- Include 17 when game station materials are submitted.
- Include 18 when Jianying template promotion is submitted.
- Include 19 for collection/合集 games, mixed gameplay packs, mini-game boxes, or materials that may not match real gameplay.
- Include 20 when the user asks about agency operation or third-party代运营.
- Always include 21 and 22 for ordinary first submission or version update because sidebar revisit is a required ability for Douyin mini-games.
